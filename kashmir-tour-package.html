KASHMIR TOUR PACKAGE

Code : NH 02
Days : 03 Nights/04 Days
Category : North Indian packages
Destination : Delhi
Delhi

Covered Locations

  • Delhi
                                                                                   
 
 
Day 01-SRINAGAR
 
On arrival at Srinagar you will be met by our representative and transfer you to Hotel. Afternoon free for independent activities. In the evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake to enjoy the sunset.
 
Dinner overnight in Hotel.
 
DAY 02: SRINAGAR – LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
 
After breakfast Leave for Mughal Gardens visiting Nishat Bagh (Abode of pleasure) built by Asif Khan brother in law of Jehangir in 1632 AD. And Shalimar Bagh (Abode of love) built by Jehangir for his wife Empress Noor Jehan. All the gardens are situated on the bank of Dal Lake with the Zabarwan Mountains in the background. The distance from Srinagar to the said gardens is about 12 and 15 kms respectively. Enroute visit carpet-weaving factory and old Srinagar city. Half-Day Excursion to Gulmarg
 
Dinner overnight in Hotel
 
DAY 03: SRINAGAR – SONMARG - SRINAGAR
 
After breakfast full day excursion to Sonamarg (Golden Meadow) .The distance of about 83 kms will be covered about 2-½ hrs. Sonamarg lies in Sindh Valley streamed with flowers and surrounded by mountains. Sonamarg is also the base for some interesting treks to the high altitude of Himalayan Lakes. Enjoy horse riding upto Thajwas glacier and sledge riding on snow. Late afternoon drive back to Srinagar
Dinner overnight in Hotel.
 
DAY 04: DEPARTURE
 
After breakfast check out from the hotel and transfer to Srinagar airport.
 
 

Rates includes:-
 
1.       Accommodation at Hotel Paradise Gateway/Zabarwan/Mumtaz Srinagar (03 Nights).
2.       03 Breakfast & 03 Dinners
3.       Transportation by 01 Non AC Tavera/Scorpio for arrival/departure transfers, halfday Mughal Gardens with Sharankacharya Temple, full day Sonamarg.
4.       Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
5.       All taxes.
6.       Assistance on arrival.
 
Rates does not include any air/train fare, meals not specified, laundry, tips, table drinks, camera/video fee, entrance fee of Mughal gardens,  personal expenses of clients or any other expenses incurred beyond our control such as bad weather, landslides, road closure, train/flight cancellation  political disturbances, etc.
