Destination : Munnar
Munnar is a hill station on the Western Ghats, a range of mountains situated in the Idukki district of the Indian state of Kerala.

 MUNNAR – THEKKADY – ALLEPPEY  TOUR PACKAGE
 
Major destiny’s  which the tour covers
·         Mattuppetty dam
·         Kundala lake
·         Echo point
·         Eravikulam National  park
·         Periyar wildlife sanctuary
·         Alleppy Houseboat cruise
 
Day 1 Cochin-Munnar
On the day you alight in Cochin international Airport/ Railway station, meet our executive to receive you. Later you will be taken to Munnar hills (130 Kms) the summer Paradise of British with unending expands of Tea plantations, spice plantations and a variety of flora and fauna. Check in your hill resort and go for sightseeing to Mattupetty Dam; a beautiful spot for viewing the picturesque Munnar, Kundala Lake- a natural lake with recreational facilities like boating and having excellent view of Western Ghats as well as the forest surrounded with.  Also visit Eco point and tea plantations. Overnight stay.
Day 2 Munnar – Thekkady
Today after breakfast checkout your hotel and move to Thekkady , the wild life destination of Kerala. You can have boating in the lake which is located in the Periyar wildlife sanctuary. While boating you may see wild animals such as elephant, bison, wild boar etc... After boating you can go for optional activities like Kathakali, which is a traditional dance dram originated in Kerala and Kalaripayattu is a martial art with lot of weapons. Overnight stay in the hotel.
Day 3 Thekkady –Alleppey (Houseboat)
Today morning  after breakfast check out your hotel and proceed to the Venice of the east (Alleppey) to board your world known traditional houseboat (Kettuvallam) to enjoy a ever memorable back water cruise through the beautifull backwater extend of Alleppey .enjoy the backwater Coir  villages and also the traditional cuisine of Alleppey . Overnight stay in the houseboat.
DAY 04 Alleppey –Cochin Drop off
Today check out your kerala houseboat by 09:00 am then proceed to Cochin to drop off.
The tour ends with sweet memmories………………………………………………………………………………………………..

KOVALAM KANYAKUMARI PACKAGE
Major destiny’s  which the tour covers
·         Kovalam beach
·         Padmanabha swami temple
·         Art gallery and zoo
·         Padmanabhapuram palace
·         Kanyakumari beach
        Day 1 Trivandrum- Kovalm
        Our tour Executive will meet and greet you at Trivandrum International Airport/Railway Station on your arrival for the Kerala Tour. Later proceed to Kovalam. Kovalam attracts large number of international beach lovers since last several decades with its magnificent sandy beach stretches and shallow water that are perfect for water sports and swimming. You can check in your beach resort and rest of the day at leisure/ enjoy the thrilling beach. Overnight stay.
        Day 2 Kovalm
        Today you can go for a half day city tour to experience the capital city of old Travancore Kingdom and presently of Kerala State. So many iconic monuments & attractions like Napier Museum, KuthiraMalika, Sri Padmanabhapuram Temple, Sri Chithira Arts Gallery, and Zoo etc are attracting travellers to here. You can enjoy the day with the vibrant colours of Trivandrum city. Later back to your beach for the unlimited fun again. Overnight stay
 Day 3 Kovalam- Kanyakumari (100 Kms/ 3 Hrs)
After breakfast drive towards Kanyakumari also known as Cape Camorine. It’s the southernmost point of India where the three seas join together as Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal and Great Indian Ocean. It’s a holy destination for Hindus with famous Devi Kanyakumari Temple & Vivekananda Rock. On the way to Kanyakumari you can visit Padhmanabha swami Palace; an example 0of typical Kerala architecture. After reaching Kanyakumari check in your hotel and evening enjoy the Sunset also have an amazing boating to the Vivekananda Rock situated in the Sea. Overnight stay in kovalam.
Day 4 kovalam - Trivandrum Drop off
Before noon check out the resort and return drop off at Trivandrum International Airport/ Railway Station for your onward journey.
