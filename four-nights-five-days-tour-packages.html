4 NIGHTS 5 DAYS TOUR PACKAGES

Code : BMH45
Days : 04 Nights/05 Days
Category : Kerala honeymoon packages
Destination : Munnar
Munnar is a hill station on the Western Ghats, a range of mountains situated in the Idukki district of the Indian state of Kerala.

 
MUNNAR THEKKADY ALLEPPEY TOUR PACKAGE
Major destiny’s  which the tour covers
·         MATTUPETTY
·         KUNDALA LAKE
·         ERAVIKULAM NATIONAL PARK
·         THEKKADY
·         ALLEPPEY
Day 1 Cochin-Munnar
On your Arrival meet our executive to receive you. Later you will be taken to Munnar hills (130 Kms), the summer Paradise of British with unending expands of Tea plantations, spice plantations and a variety of flora and fauna. Check in your hill resort and after noon at leisure/ you can go to visit Munnar town and nearby places & Overnight stay.
Day 2 Munnar Sightseeing
Today after breakfast, go for sightseeing at Mattupetty Dam; a beautiful spot for viewing the picturesque Munnar, Kundala Lake- a natural lake with recreational facilities like boating and having excellent view of Western Ghats as well as the forest surrounded with.  Also visit Eco point and tea plantations. After noon you can visit Eravikulam National park; where the endangering NilgiriTahr is conserved. Later drive back to your resort for Overnight stay.
Day 3 Munnar – Thekkady
Today after breakfast checkout your hotel and move to Thekkady , the wild life destination of Kerala. You can have boating in the lake which is located in the Periyar wildlife sanctuary. While boating you may see wild animals such as elephant, bison, wild boar etc... After boating you can go for optional activities like Kathakali, which is a traditional dance dram originated in Kerala and Kalaripayattu is a martial art with lot of weapons. Overnight stay in the hotel.
Day 4 Thekkady – Alleppey(houseboat)
After breakfast Check out your resort and drive to Alleppey; also known as Venice of the east by its special geographical features as the place is famous for its vast back water stretches and paddy fields. By 12 pm you can board your house boat to explore the back waters and its serene beauty. Enjoy the ride; delicious cuisines and an overnight stay onboard
Day 5  Alleppey-  drop off.
After having breakfast from the house boat, check out to drive towards Cochin international Airport/ Railway station for your onward journey.
