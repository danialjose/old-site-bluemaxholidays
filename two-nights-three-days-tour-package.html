CALICUT – WAYANAD TOUR PACKAGE

 

 
Major destiny’s  which the tour covers

·         Pazhassiraja Muesium
·         Art Gallery
·         Kappad Beach
·         Edakkal Caves
·         Banasura Dam
·         Pookode lake
 
DAY 01 CALICUT INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT ARRIVAL
On the day of you alight in Calicut our representative welcome you to Gods own county and then proceed to your hotel in Kappad  one of the major beach destination in kerala , which is incomparable with its calm and clean atmosphere blended with the sun and sands. Check in your beach resort by  noon proceed to Calicut town for sightseeing visit the world famous pazhassiraja museum and the art gallery  (Monday holiday) then proceed to enjoy the beach games and sunset later back to your beach resort over night stay .
DAY 02 CALICUT - WAYANAD
Today morning check out your resort and proceed to the cool hill station of Kerala Wayanad,after check in your resort  go for sightseeing to Edakkal caves which is famous for the wall paintings ,sculptures and old rock scripts which highlights the rich cultural background of kerala. Then after that proceed to Banasura dam Asias second largest earth dam by evening visit the pokode lake have some leisure and enjoy the optional activities like boating and park walk etc later back to your resort and overnight stay in the cool spot of north kerala.
DAY 03 WAYANAD –CALICUT DROP OFF
Today morning check out your hotel and return back to Calicut for return drop.
